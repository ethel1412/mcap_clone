Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

MCAP is a unified online platform developed by the State Higher Education Council, Government of Meghalaya to streamline undergraduate admissions across participating government colleges.

MCAP aims to provide a transparent, efficient, and student-friendly admission process by allowing students to apply to multiple institutions through a single application.

Students seeking admission to undergraduate programmes in participating government colleges in Meghalaya can apply through the portal.

In its initial phase, MCAP includes 8 Government Colleges in Meghalaya.

No. Students only need to submit one application through which they can apply to multiple participating institutions.

Admissions are offered under the Four-Year Undergraduate Programme (FYUGP), aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP).

Admissions are merit-based and depend on the eligibility criteria defined by each participating institution.

Yes. Each institution may have its own eligibility and merit criteria, but all applications are processed through the same portal.

The portal provides timely updates and notifications regarding application status, merit lists, and seat allotment.

Login Instructions

  • For applicants, select User Type as "Applicant".
  • Enter your registered mobile number or registered email ID as the username.
  • Enter your password and Date of Birth (DOB) to login.
  • Please read the Complete User Manual carefully before login.
  • For assistance, please refer to the Contact Us page and contact the concerned SPOC Officer / Helpdesk between 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM.

Applicant Fee Structure

The application fee is determined based on your category and permanent residence.

Application fee structure for MCAP applicants
Applicant Category Fee (₹)
SC / ST — Permanent Residents of Meghalaya ₹ 200
OBC / General — Residents of Meghalaya ₹ 500
Applicants from Outside Meghalaya ₹ 1,000

Residency is verified based on your Domicile Certificate or Permanent Address in Meghalaya.

Instructions for Preference Selection
  • Select a Stream, Programme, Institute, and Shift, then click "Add to List" to save your preference.
  • You may add up to 20 preferences across multiple streams (for example, Science, Commerce, and Arts).
  • Arrange your preferences carefully using the drag-and-drop handle. Preference #1 will be treated as your highest priority during seat allotment.
  • Ensure that your preferences are submitted in the correct order before final submission, as allotment will be processed based on the order of preferences selected by you.

My Application Journey

Please complete the steps below sequentially. You will be able to add multiple programmes and preferences in Step 3.

Step 1: Personal Details

Provide your basic identity, contact, and family information.

Step 2: Academic Details

Enter your past school marks and entrance exam scores.

Step 3: Programme Preferences

Select and add the streams, institutes, and programmes you wish to apply for.

Step 4: Document Upload

Upload your photograph, signature, and mandatory certificates.

Step 5: Payment & Submission

Pay the requisite fees to lock and finalize your profile.

Step 6: Seat Allotment Result

Check if you have been allotted a seat after the merit list is published.

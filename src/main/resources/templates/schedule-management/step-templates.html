How to build the Roadmap

The Master Roadmap defines the global sequence of events for the admission cycle. It is automatically divided into phases:

  • Pre-Admission Phase: Click "Auto-Generate" to instantly add the required institute setup and approval steps.
  • Counselling Phase: Click to add CUET or Non-CUET phases. The system will automatically build the Merit, Allotment, and Payment steps for you.
  • Note: The Student Application Window is managed separately in the 'Admission Windows' menu, but acts as the anchor dividing these two phases here.
